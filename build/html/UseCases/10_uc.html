Goal
Coordinating Node checks “liveness” of all Member Nodes - checks ping, service x, load, space, bandwidth, transaction rate, ...

Summary

As part of the infrastructure monitoring process, a CN checks on the “liveness” of all Member Nodes. This operation will be performed with moderate frequency (perhaps in the order of once every few minutes). The basic operation of pinging the MN to test for connectivity.
Actors
CN, MN

Preconditions

  • CNs are operational
  • MN is registered

Triggers

  • Timed interval
  • Registration change

Post Conditions

  • Status metadata is updated at CNs
